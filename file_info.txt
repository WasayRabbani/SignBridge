1. check_video_correct: checks whether video is correctly shot. Checks video hands,pose landmarks

2. checking_raw_coordinates_validity: Shows video's numpy array in video form like coordinates.

3. checking_sequence: checks average sequence number of all videos. Helps in preprocessing, model training and testing

4. checking_short_vid: checks which videos are short and can disturb model training

5. detailed_test: checks all single videos in one time of all signs. (Did not check it)

6. extract_raw_coordinates: extracts raw coordinates from mp4 video and save them in numpy array. One video, one array

7. model_training: Trains BILSTM model

8. multi_sign_predict: Here we give a video with multiple signs and it extarcts signs and give sentence.

9. preprocess_test: Preprocesses test set. Last 20%. Does the minimum needed

10. preprocess_train: Preprocesses training set. First 80% complete

11. rename_files: Renames videos in sequence for each word

12. single_vid_test: Tests single sign video

13. split_nothing_class: Make a long video for nothing class. Splits it into mutiple short videos
